Our Story

TERRA was born from a simple yet profound realization: the fashion industry didn't have to be at odds with the planet. Founded in a small studio in Portland, Oregon, we set out to prove that sustainability and style could coexist beautifully.

Today, we work with artisan communities across four continents, pioneering new sustainable materials while honoring traditional craftsmanship. Every stitch carries our commitment to a more conscious future.
